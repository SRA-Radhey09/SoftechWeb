Company Profile

Softech Consultants provides advisory services in all products/Projects setups, unit setups under SEZ, STPI, EHTP & EOU, and all other liaison with Govt. authorities. Our Estd. is 1993 and have overall experience of 23 years.

We are consultants for Custom / Central Excise related matters of SEZ, STPI, EHTP & 100% EOU Scheme registration . We undertake liaison, Representation and other legal formalities.

We also have excellent expertise in setting up Projects & providing various services related to duty free imports and other duty exemption day-to-day regulatory matters.

Our Knowledge & experience has been helpful to all our clients, who are very much happy & satisfied with our consultancy services & it has helped them to reduce their cost of product and to save valuable time. Once they appoint us they need not appoint any other agency separately for the SEZ/STP/EHTP/EOU formalities including administrative services as we provide one window clearance facility.

We also undertake the clearing forwarding facilities under one roof, which clears your import cargo and delivers it to your unit / factory safely.
